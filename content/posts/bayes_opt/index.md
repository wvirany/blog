## Background

* Goal is to maximize some function $f$, i.e., find

$$
\begin{align*}
x^* \in \arg \max_{x \in \mathcal{X}}
\end{align*}
$$

* $f$ need not be analytic (i.e., can't write it down), nor computable - we just need some access to information at specified points
